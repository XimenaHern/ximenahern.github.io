Videos

On this page I would like to showcase some of my favorite web design/CSS videos. These videos share tips, advice, and some helpful resources to improve designs.

This video explains different key components of design and offers various tips on color, contrast, the use of whitespace, symmetry and harmony.

The video offers 4 websites that users can get inspiration from for designing websites. Siteinspire.com displays many sites to give viewers ideas. Web design-inspiration.com is similar woth the added ability to view designs on desktop,mobile, and tablet to ompare how the design could look on various devices. Awwwards.com showcases winners and nominees from web design competitions that can be viewed by the public. Templatemonster.com sells templates and webdite themes but can also be used to browse through for inspiration. All these sites are interactive and allow people to visit the live sites and explore the features, elements, and experience each design in full. Looking at websites like these is good for studying design principles and learn new techniques, methods, themes, and styles to elevate design abilities.

Multiple tips and pieces of advice for navigating the use of CSS are offered, my favorites include learning CSS fundamentals, learning the box model, flexbox, and grid which help with alignment and layout of elements on sites. Firefox has useful tools for debugging that chrome does not have, and he exlpains the importance and how to use global variables to make editing easier and time-effecient.
